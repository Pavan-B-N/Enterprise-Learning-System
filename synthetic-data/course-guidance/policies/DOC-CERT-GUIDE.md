## Purpose

This guide defines the certification pathways for all engineering roles within the organisation. It maps each role to primary and secondary certifications, outlines recommended study patterns, and establishes readiness criteria for exam scheduling.

## Role-to-Certification Mapping

### Cloud Engineer (ROLE-CE)
- **Primary Certification:** AZ-204 (Azure Developer Associate)
- **Secondary Certification:** AZ-104 (Azure Administrator Associate)
- **Advanced Path:** AZ-305 (Azure Solutions Architect Expert)
- **Foundational Prerequisite:** AZ-900 (recommended before AZ-204)

### DevOps Engineer (ROLE-DE)
- **Primary Certification:** AZ-400 (DevOps Engineer Expert)
- **Prerequisites:** AZ-104 OR AZ-204 (one required before attempting AZ-400)
- **Secondary:** AZ-204 (for development-focused DevOps)

### Data Engineer (ROLE-DA)
- **Primary Certification:** DP-203 (Data Engineer Associate)
- **Foundational:** AZ-900 (recommended)
- **Extended Path:** Consider AZ-204 for data application development

### Security Engineer (ROLE-SE)
- **Primary Certification:** AZ-500 (Azure Security Engineer Associate)
- **Prerequisite:** AZ-104 (recommended for security context)
- **Advanced Path:** AZ-305 (for security architecture focus)

### AI Engineer (ROLE-AI)
- **Primary Certification:** AI-102 (Azure AI Engineer Associate)
- **Secondary:** AZ-204 (for AI application integration)
- **Foundational:** AZ-900

### Solutions Architect (ROLE-SA)
- **Primary Certification:** AZ-305 (Azure Solutions Architect Expert)
- **Prerequisites:** AZ-104 (mandatory) + AZ-204 (recommended)
- **Extended:** AZ-500 (for security-focused architecture)

## Recommended Study Patterns

### Daily Study Approach
- **Optimal duration:** 1–2 hours of focused study per day
- **Minimum effective session:** 30 minutes for retention
- **Maximum before diminishing returns:** 3 hours without break
- **Best timing:** During identified focus windows (see Work IQ signals)

### Weekly Checkpoints
- Complete at least one practice assessment per week after week 2
- Review weak areas identified in previous assessments
- Update study plan if score trends show consistent gaps

### Readiness Indicators
- **Ready for exam:** Practice score consistently ≥ 75% across 3+ attempts
- **Borderline:** Practice score 65-74% — recommend 1-2 more weeks
- **Not ready:** Practice score < 65% — revisit fundamentals, extend timeline

## Assessment and Progression Rules

1. Learners should not schedule an exam until achieving ≥ 75% on practice assessments
2. A minimum of 2 practice assessments is required before exam booking
3. If a learner fails an exam, a 14-day cooling period applies before the next attempt
4. Managers receive visibility into team readiness but not individual scores (privacy)
5. Study plans automatically extend by 2 weeks if milestone completion drops below 60%

## Responsible Learning Guidelines

- Study time should not conflict with critical delivery commitments
- Managers should ensure team members have adequate focus time allocated
- Certification goals should align with role expectations and career growth
- No learner should feel pressured to certify faster than their capacity allows
